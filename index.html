Steps To Make A Lassi:

1.First add 50g Yougurt to the glass

2.Then Squeeze/Juice The Fruit Which You want to be the flavour

3.Add 90ml milk to the glass

4.Add 5ml Lime/Lemon Juice

5.Add 50g Sugar

6. Mix It well Until It turns to a liquid

7.Decorate it with mint on the top
